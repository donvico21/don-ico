The Artist

About

Don Ico has been playing guitar since age 10, moving through bands over the years — Anima Creed, Pennies 4 Jane, Downtools, Bazuka Joes, Sancho's Punani, and others — each one adding to a range of influences and collaborations that would eventually shape his own voice.

Now writing and recording as a solo artist, Don Ico channels the raw frustration of the Filipino diaspora into protest anthems that blend indie rock, ska, and punk. His music confronts disinformation, systemic corruption, democratic erosion, and the ongoing fight for truth in the Philippines — not from despair, but from urgent, unflinching hope.

Releases are DIY at their core: name-your-price downloads, collaborative recordings with musicians across the Philippines and beyond, and a commitment to keeping music a tool for accountability.
